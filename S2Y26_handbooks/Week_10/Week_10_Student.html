MOCK EXAM QUESTIONS

Work through these four questions. They represent the style and difficulty of the actual exam Part II.

Question 1: Hypothesis Testing (12 marks)

A pharmaceutical company claims their new painkiller reduces headache duration by at least 30% compared to standard treatment. A clinical trial with 50 patients shows: - Mean reduction: 28% - Standard deviation: 8%

  1. State the null and alternative hypotheses (2 marks)

  2. Calculate the test statistic (4 marks)

  3. At significance level \(\alpha = 0.05\), should the company’s claim be rejected? (4 marks)

  4. What does your conclusion mean for patients? (2 marks)

Question 2: Optimization in Context (12 marks)

A dairy farm produces milk at a cost of \(C(x) = 2x^2 - 80x + 1200\) dollars, where \(x\) is thousands of liters produced per week. Milk sells for $1.20 per liter.

  1. Write the profit function \(P(x)\) (3 marks)

  2. Find the production level that maximizes profit (5 marks)

  3. What is the maximum weekly profit? (2 marks)

  4. If fuel costs increase and the cost function becomes \(C(x) = 2x^2 - 80x + 1500\), how does optimal production change? (2 marks)

Question 3: Integration Application (12 marks)

A solar panel’s power output (in watts) varies throughout the day according to: \[P(t) = 500\sin\left(\frac{\pi t}{12}\right)\] where \(t\) is hours after sunrise (0 ≤ t ≤ 12).

  1. What is the peak power output and when does it occur? (3 marks)

  2. Calculate the total energy produced during the day (energy = integral of power): \[E = \int_0^{12} P(t)\,dt\] (6 marks)

  3. Express your answer in kilowatt-hours (kWh) (1 kWh = 1000 watt-hours) (2 marks)

  4. At $0.30 per kWh, what is the daily revenue from this panel? (1 mark)

Question 4: Differential Equations (12 marks)

A water tank drains according to the differential equation: \[\frac{dV}{dt} = -0.5\sqrt{V}\] where \(V\) is volume (liters) and \(t\) is time (minutes). Initially, \(V(0) = 100\) liters.

  1. Separate variables and integrate to find \(V(t)\) (6 marks)

  2. How long until the tank is half empty? (3 marks)

  3. How long until the tank is completely empty? (2 marks)

  4. Sketch \(V(t)\) vs. \(t\) (1 mark)
