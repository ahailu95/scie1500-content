Then try opening the app again.

Make sure you extracted the ZIP first (right-click ‚Üí Extract All). Running from inside the ZIP archive will not work.

The free AI tutor requires an internet connection. Check your Wi-Fi and try again. If the issue persists, the service may be temporarily busy ‚Äî wait a moment and retry.
